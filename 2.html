EPITECH

The Paris Graduate School of Digital Innovation (French: École pour l'informatique et les nouvelles technologies, or EPITECH), formerly European Institute of Information Technology, is a private institution of higher education in computer science that was founded in 1999.[2][3]

Headquartered in Le Kremlin-Bicêtre, south of Paris, the school has campuses in Bordeaux, Rennes, Marseille, Lille, Lyon, Montpellier, Nancy, Nantes, Nice, Strasbourg, Toulouse and Saint-André, Réunion.[4] The school also has locations in Barcelona (Spain),[5] Tirana (Albania),[6] Berlin (Germany), [7] and Brussels (Belgium).[8]

The school has the particularity to teach with practical cases instead of theoretical.[9] Epitech has also an Executive MBA in IT and entrepreneurship course targeting executive managers in computer science.[10] The institution is part of IONIS Education Group.
